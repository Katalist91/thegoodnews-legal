Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

TheGoodNews World Wide ("we", "us", or "our") operates social media accounts on platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our content publishing activities.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from viewers or followers. Our automated publishing system accesses only the platform APIs necessary to post video content to our own accounts. We do not store or process any data about third parties.

2. Use of Platform APIs

We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish video content to our own TikTok account (@thegoodnews_world_wide). We access only the following scopes:

  • video.publish – to post videos to our account
  • user.info.basic – to verify our own account identity

We do not access, collect, or store any data from other TikTok users.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and API credentials are stored locally on our private server and are never shared with third parties. No user data is stored, processed, or transmitted to any external service.

4. Third-Party Platforms

Our content is published to third-party platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube). Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle data. We encourage you to review those policies directly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Facebook Page.
